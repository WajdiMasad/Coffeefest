02 — Logo System

Primary Mark

The Official Logo

The Coffee Fest Halifax logo is the cornerstone of our brand identity. It combines maritime heritage — represented through the anchor and ocean waves — with the warmth and craft of specialty coffee culture. The circular seal format conveys a sense of occasion, tradition, and community.

Logo Variations

Primary — On Navy
Secondary — On Cream

Clear Space

Always maintain a minimum clear space equal to the height of the "C" in COFFEE around all sides of the logo. Never crowd the logo with other elements.

Accessibility Note

Admiral Navy (#192E41) on Sea Cream (#E8DCC8) achieves a contrast ratio of 8.4:1 — exceeding WCAG AA and AAA standards. Maritime Gold (#C8A44E) should only be used as an accent colour, not for body text on white backgrounds.

05 — Brand Voice & Usage

Brand Personality

Voice & Tone

Warm & Welcoming

We speak to everyone — from the casual coffee drinker to the dedicated connoisseur. Our tone is approachable, enthusiastic, and never condescending.

Proud & Local

Halifax is not a backdrop — it's the soul of the event. We celebrate Nova Scotia's community, coast, and culture in everything we say and create.

Premium, Not Pretentious

We uphold high standards for craft and quality, but we communicate them with humility and joy. Great coffee should feel exciting, not exclusive.

Celebratory

Coffee Fest is a festival. Our language should feel alive, energetic, and worth looking forward to. Think: invitation, not announcement.

Logo Usage Rules

Do
  • Use the logo on approved backgrounds (navy, cream, white, espresso)
  • Maintain the minimum clear space on all sides
  • Use the full-colour version wherever possible
  • Scale the logo proportionally at all times
  • Request the source .ai file for print production
  • Use the logo at a minimum size of 30mm wide in print
Don't
  • Stretch, skew, or distort the logo in any dimension
  • Apply drop shadows, glows, or unsanctioned effects
  • Place the logo on busy photographic backgrounds
  • Change any colours within the logo mark
  • Recreate or redraw the logo in another application
  • Use the logo at sizes below minimum clear space requirements
