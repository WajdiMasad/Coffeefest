6. The Company is authorized to issue an unlimited number of shares divided into the following classes:
   
   ### (a) Class A Common Shares
   An unlimited number of Class A Common Shares without nominal or par value, having the following rights, privileges, restrictions, and conditions:
   * **Voting:** The holders of Class A Common Shares shall be entitled to receive notice of and to attend all meetings of the shareholders of the Company and shall have one (1) vote for each Class A Common Share held at all such meetings.
   * **Dividends:** The holders of Class A Common Shares shall be entitled to receive dividends as and when declared by the Board of Directors, out of the funds of the Company properly available for the payment of dividends, in such rate and in such manner as the Board of Directors may from time to time determine. The Board of Directors may, in its absolute discretion, declare dividends on the Class A Common Shares to the exclusion of any other class of shares, or vice-versa.
   * **Liquidation, Dissolution, or Winding-Up:** In the event of the liquidation, dissolution, or winding-up of the Company, or other distribution of assets of the Company among its shareholders for the purpose of winding up its affairs, the holders of Class A Common Shares shall be entitled to receive, subject to the rights of the holders of any other class of shares ranking senior, the remaining property and assets of the Company, sharing equally, share for share, with the holders of Class B Common Shares.

   ### (b) Class B Common Shares
   An unlimited number of Class B Common Shares without nominal or par value, having the following rights, privileges, restrictions, and conditions:
   * **Non-Voting:** Except as otherwise required by the Nova Scotia *Companies Act* or by law, the holders of Class B Common Shares shall not, as such, be entitled to receive notice of, to attend, or to vote at any meetings of the shareholders of the Company.
   * **Dividends:** The holders of Class B Common Shares shall be entitled to receive dividends as and when declared by the Board of Directors, out of the funds of the Company properly available for the payment of dividends, in such rate and in such manner as the Board of Directors may from time to time determine. The Board of Directors may, in its absolute discretion, declare dividends on the Class B Common Shares to the exclusion of any other class of shares, or vice-versa.
   * **Liquidation, Dissolution, or Winding-Up:** In the event of the liquidation, dissolution, or winding-up of the Company, or other distribution of assets of the Company among its shareholders for the purpose of winding up its affairs, the holders of Class B Common Shares shall be entitled to receive, subject to the rights of the holders of any other class of shares ranking senior, the remaining property and assets of the Company, sharing equally, share for share, with the holders of Class A Common Shares.
