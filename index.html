The Festival

A Celebration of Craft & Culture

Coffee Fest Halifax is the East Coast's first world-class coffee festival — a one-day immersive experience where artisan roasters, champion baristas, and coffee lovers converge on the Halifax waterfront.

From single-origin tastings and latte art throwdowns to masterclass workshops on sustainability and sourcing, every moment is curated for the curious palate and the passionate enthusiast.

What to Expect

An Unforgettable Experience

One unforgettable day of discovery, connection, and the finest coffee culture Atlantic Canada has ever seen.

01 🫘

World-Class Tastings

Sample exceptional single-origin coffees from over 50 artisan roasters across Canada and beyond. From Ethiopian Yirgacheffe to Colombian Gesha — every cup tells a story.

02 🏆

Latte Art Throwdown

Watch the East Coast's finest baristas compete in a live latte art championship. Precision, creativity, and showmanship collide in one of the festival's most electrifying events.

03 🎓

Masterclass Workshops

Learn the art of the pour-over, espresso extraction, and home roasting from industry legends. Whether you're a curious beginner or a seasoned professional, there's a seat for you.

04 🍽️

Culinary Pairings

Discover the surprising harmony of coffee and food. Local chefs and roasters collaborate to create tasting experiences that challenge everything you thought you knew about flavour.

05 🎶

Live Music & Culture

The festival comes alive with East Coast artists, ambient sets, and curated sounds that perfectly complement your coffee journey from morning brew to evening espresso.

06 🌊

Halifax Waterfront

Set against the backdrop of one of Canada's most iconic harbours, Coffee Fest Halifax transforms the waterfront into a world-class cultural destination for one spectacular day.

Why Halifax?

Halifax is more than a backdrop — it's a character in the story. A city of salt air, maritime history, and a thriving independent food and coffee scene, Halifax was the only choice for Atlantic Canada's first world-class coffee festival.
