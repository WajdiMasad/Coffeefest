Dear Prospective Partner,

Re: Sponsorship Opportunity — Coffee Fest Halifax  |  October 3, 2026

On behalf of Coffee Fest Halifax, we are pleased to extend this invitation to your organization to become an official sponsor of Atlantic Canada's first coffee festival, taking place on October 3, 2026 at the Halifax Waterfront, managed by Build Nova Scotia. Coffee Fest Halifax is produced by the same organizing team behind two of the region's most successful community events — East Coast Inflate-a-Palooza and Philippine Fiesta — bringing a proven record of large-scale event execution, community engagement, and sponsorship delivery to everything we undertake.

Coffee Fest Halifax is a one-day festival experience bringing together twenty of the region's finest artisan roasters, local cafés, bakeries, culinary artists, and coffee enthusiasts from across the Maritimes and beyond. Programming includes live brewing competitions, culinary pairings, and live performances — all set against the backdrop of one of the most celebrated waterfronts on the East Coast.

Why This Opportunity Matters

The specialty coffee industry represents a significant and rapidly growing consumer market defined by brand loyalty, discernment, and a deep appreciation for quality. Our projected attendance of five thousand guests over the festival weekend will draw a diverse audience of passionate consumers, industry professionals, and destination visitors — an audience that is educated, engaged, and fiercely brand-conscious.

Sponsoring Coffee Fest Halifax places your organization within a premium shared experience, activating your brand across the official website and digital channels, a curated subscriber email audience, organic and paid social media campaigns, on-site venue signage, live announcements, and regional media coverage.
